Features

With Diggr scanning and uploading your record inventory is easier than ever. Just let our album recognition technology scan the cover and we'll take care of the rest. All of a sudden hundreds, even THOUSANDS of albums can be inventoried in a few afternoons.

When you use Diggr, you not only get access to some rad inventory technology, you can also take advantage of our online store. Gone are the days of trying to figure out web design and hosting. With your inventory in the Diggr database, setting up your digital storefront is as easy as 1-2-3!

It's never been easier to organize and curate your inventory with Diggr. Separate your albums by artist, genre, or decade. Show off curated collections such as staff picks, choices of the week, or rare and collectible albums. With Diggr, the choice is yours and the possibilities endless. You get to decide how your shop is organized.

You're never far from your collection with the Diggr app. Use the app to intake and organize albums, manage inventory, set prices, and oversee your online shop. Use the Diggr website for full access to your merchant dashboard. Whether at your desk or on the go, Diggr stays with you!
